# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Single-owner local business operators or their trusted ops assistant, usually triaging Google Business Profile reviews between customer work sessions and often from a phone.

## Product Purpose
Review Pilot helps an owner connect Google locations, generate review reply drafts, publish or mark reviews handled, and receive Twilio task links for time-sensitive responses. Success means fewer missed reviews, faster confident replies, and a local self-hosted setup that keeps sensitive account credentials under the owner's control.

## Brand Personality
Calm, capable, precise. The product should feel like a dependable operations cockpit: quiet enough for repeated use, polished enough to trust with customer-facing replies, and direct enough to work well on mobile.

## Anti-references
Avoid marketing-site hero styling, decorative dashboards, oversized SaaS cards, dense desktop-only admin screens, and fragile mobile layouts where primary actions fall below the fold or compete with setup noise.

## Design Principles
Make the next action obvious on every screen.
Favor mobile reachability for review handling and notification workflows.
Separate setup, queue triage, and operational task management into clear surfaces.
Use familiar product UI patterns so the owner trusts the tool quickly.
Expose risk and status plainly before publishing customer-facing replies.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Aim for WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-accessible forms and actions, visible focus states, reduced-motion-safe transitions, and controls that remain large enough for mobile touch use.
